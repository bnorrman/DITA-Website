Bond Representation Attorneys

The Advocates for Human Rights needs bond representation attorneys to assist detained immigrants with their legal process.

What do bond representation attorneys do?

Bond representation attorneys represent detained immigrants during their removal proceedings.

Why are bond representation attorneys important?

People detained by ICE have a right to legal counsel, but not to a government-appointed attorney. Many remain in custody because they cannot afford an attorney. Having a bond representation attorney makes a big difference for the detained, and represented immigrants are four times more likely to be released from detention.

What are the requirements to volunteer as a bond representation attorney?

You need a license to practice law in a U.S. jurisdiction, but you do not need specialized knowledge about immigration.
